Section: Part II:  General Linux Exam 102


Chapter 22. Exam 102 Review Questions and Exercises

This section presents review questions to highlight important concepts and hands-on exercises that you can use to gain experience with the Topics covered on the LPI's Exam 102. The exercises can be particularly useful if you're not accustomed to routine Linux administration and should help you better prepare for the exam. To complete the exercises, you need a working Linux system that is not in production use.
