Chapter 9. Exam 101 Practice Test

Exam 101 consists approximately of 60 questions. Most are multiple-choice single-answer, a few are multiple-choice multiple-answer, and the remainder are fill-in questions. No notes or other materials are permitted, and you have 90 minutes to complete the exam. The answers are provided (Section 9.2).
